Andre Horton

I've been taking my stab at this thing called 'Design' for 8 years now. I received a Bachelor of Arts degree in Graphic design from CSU Fresno. I have experience in Illustration, Branding, Print Design, UI/UX, Motion Graphics, Video Production & Product Design.

Hi there, my name is Andre and I’m a graphic designer & illustrator based in London. I have a passion for all things art and design and I’m always striving to learn new design techniques to further expand my skill set. In my spare time when I’m not working on freelance projects or taking part in design competitions I enjoy creating things that reflect my interests and experiences.

If you would like to work with me or are just passing through and want to say hi – please feel free to get in contact with me.
