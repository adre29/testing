Dyna-Micah

Album Cover

My design entry was chosen for conscious Hip-Hop artist ‘Dyna-MicAH’s’ debut EP 'InFromation’.

The client was looking for an eye catching Album Cover that not only stood out at first glance but on closer inspection creatively captivated the viewers imagination.

Alongside a list of design requirements he supplied us with photo’s of himself and a rough sketch of what he was looking for which I used as a guide during the creative process. I decided to create an illustrative album cover instead of using photo imagery as it allowed me to to create something that had a personal touch but it also enabled me to be more imaginative with the colour scheme.
